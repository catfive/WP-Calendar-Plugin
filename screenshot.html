Program Leader | Executive-in-Residence | Dan Balan

Dan Balan is a bender of shapes and shifter of paradigms. He is widely recognized as a global thought leader in transforming corporations. Author of two books, his second book Unleash: The 8 Forces that Transform Companies is due for publication in June 2011. In this book he lays out a new framework for transforming the global economy. He has advised scores of global companies like Pepsi, Tropicana, Frito-Lay, GE aircraft engines, Rockwell, Wrigley, Motorola, and several others spanning multiple industries.

Dan has served as executive-in-residence and as a visiting professor at IIT's Stuart Graduate School of Business where he started the supply chain program. He has spoken at multiple national summits, and his work on supply chains and innovation has been adopted by industry-leading associations. He is the head of a Chicago-based advisory company, Intraqq Consulting Group. He can be reached at dbalan@intraqq.com.
